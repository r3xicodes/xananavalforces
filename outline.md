## Page Sections

### 1. index.html - Homepage
- **Header**: Fixed navigation with Imperial Naval Seal
- **Hero Section**: Full-width background with overlay headline
- **Quick Information Bar**: Four horizontal panels
- **Featured Content Grid**: Three-card layout
- **Footer**: Imperial seal, legal notice, contact info

### 2. about.html - About Section
- Mission statement
- Legal Authority under Xaiar Law
- Role within the Empire
- Relationship between XNN and RXN
- Collapsible panels for each subsection

### 3. leadership.html - Leadership Page
- High Admiral profile
- Deputy High Admirals
- Chiefs of Naval Staff
- Official portraits with institutional biographies
- Clear marking of Crown-commanded positions

### 4. fleets.html - Fleets & Vessels
- Filterable fleet index
- Filter by: Fleet type, Operational status, Region
- Vessel fact sheets (USAF-style layout)
- Class Name, Commission Year, Role, Home Fleet, Status
- No tactical specifications

### 5. operations.html - Operations
- Active Operations (Limited Disclosure)
- Training & Exercises
- Joint Operations
- Humanitarian Missions
- Public-facing dashboard with factual summaries

### 6. history.html - History Section
- Interactive timeline
- Founding Era
- Major Naval Wars
- Modernization Period
- Contemporary Era

### 7. news.html - News & Dispatches
- USAF-style newsroom layout
- Left: Article list
- Right: Filters and categories
- Filters: Issuing Authority, Fleet, Operation Type, Date
- Neutral, factual tone

### 8. careers.html - Careers
- Enlisted Service
- Officer Commissioning
- Naval Academies
- Specialized Corps
- Emphasis on duty, professionalism, service

## Technical Implementation

### Color Palette
- Deep Navy Blue: #1B2951 (primary background and headers)
- White: #FFFFFF (primary content background)
- Slate Gray: #64748B (dividers, metadata, secondary UI)
- Muted Gold: #B8860B (limited use for RXN identifiers)

### Typography
- Headings: Bold, all-caps for section headers
- Body: Neutral sans-serif for long reading
- Metadata: Smaller, spaced text

### Responsive Design
- Mobile-first approach
- High-contrast mode
- Screen-reader optimized
- Keyboard navigation
- Tablet and desktop optimization

### JavaScript Functionality
- Collapsible panels
- Filter systems
- Interactive timeline
- Mobile navigation toggle
- Accessibility features
