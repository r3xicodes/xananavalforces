Naval Leadership

High Admiralty of the Xana National Navy & Royal Xana Navy

Command Structure

The Imperial Navy operates under the unified command of the High Admiralty, comprising the High Admiral and Deputy High Admirals. This leadership structure ensures coordinated operations across both the Xana National Navy and Royal Xana Navy, maintaining the empire's maritime supremacy and operational excellence.

High Admiral

Supreme Commander of the Imperial Navy

High Admiral

Lord Admiral Marcus Strathtyran

Supreme Commander, Imperial Navy | Crown Naval Advisor

Lord Admiral Strathtyran assumed command of the Imperial Navy in 2472 following distinguished service across multiple fleet commands. With over thirty years of naval experience, he has led operations from anti-piracy patrols to major fleet engagements. His leadership has been instrumental in modernizing imperial naval capabilities and strengthening maritime alliances. Under his command, the navy has achieved unprecedented operational readiness and technological advancement.

Deputy High Admirals

Supporting the High Admiral in naval operations and administration

Deputy High Admiral

Admiral Elena Chen

Deputy Commander for Operations | XNN Fleet Coordination

Admiral Chen oversees all operational aspects of the Xana National Navy. Her expertise in fleet tactics and strategic planning has been crucial in developing modern naval doctrine. She previously commanded the 3rd Fleet and led successful anti-piracy campaigns in the Eastern Waters.

Deputy High Admiral

Admiral Heinrich Voss

Deputy Commander for Administration | RXN Special Operations

Admiral Voss manages naval administration and oversees Royal Xana Navy special operations. His background in naval intelligence and strategic planning has been vital to imperial maritime security. He directs crown naval activities and ceremonial fleet functions.
